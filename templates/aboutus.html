Brief introduction

Bluspan Professional Services "Bluspan" is a professional Tax, Regulatory and People Services firm
with a Global network that serves both local and multinational companies.
We specialize in providing tax solutions to all types of businesses stretching from conception
to execution, including a robust and maintainable tax audit defense, strategically designed to
curtail tax liabilities to the barest minimum.
Our regulatory solutions are premised on tested and proven knowledge of the regulatory horizon.
We focus on developing our people for the future.
Our practice in the short run focuses on serving the Sub-Saharan African Region with potentials
to grow into an African and global business covering Europe, Middle East and the Americas
